ABOUT THE MALL

Shopping malls are not only places to shop; they also have become places for having fun and spending time. Consumers spend a considerable time in shopping malls because they host a lot of stores and activities that attract consumers' attention and offer a lot of benefits. A shopping centre is a collection of independent retail stores, services, and a parking area conceived, constructed, and maintained by a management firm as a unit. Shopping centres may also contain restaurants, banks, theatres, professional offices, service stations, and other establishments.

There are six factors that affect a mall's success: comfort, diversity, luxury, mall essence, entertainment and convenience. These factors match up well with the criteria we at tvsdesign use when identifying needed renovations for malls that are beginning to show their age

In today's blog, we will be taking a look at the five features that will make your community shopping center stand out from the rest.
  • Walkability.
  • Water features.
  • Reflective spots.
  • Greenery in design.
  • Comfort and shade.
